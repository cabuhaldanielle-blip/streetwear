The Story

Founded by Ma. Cristina Reyes, Keep Silent is more than just a fashion label; it is a personal narrative. Born from a desire to find quiet confidence amidst the chaos of the entertainment industry, the brand represents the power of understated expression.

We specialize in "comfort-core"—essentials that feel like a second skin. Our monochrome palette and minimalist typography reflect our core belief: that true style doesn't need to shout to be heard.

Every piece is designed with intention. From the heavy-weight cottons to the subtle "shhh" motifs, we invite you to embrace the silence and find strength in it.

- Ma. Cristina Reyes
